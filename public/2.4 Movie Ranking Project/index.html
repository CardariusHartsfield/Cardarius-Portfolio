Greatest Movies Of All Time!

Top 3 movies of all-time.

Django

Django is one of the best action movies ever. Although it has some disturbing scenes and languages, the overall movie outside of those things are very rewarding. I highly recommend this movie.

The Mist

If you are lookin for a good horror movie, The Mist is a great choice. Originally written by Stephen King, The Mist will keep you on you toes for the duration of the movie.

Friday

One of the best comedies in the 90's, Friday has a great cast that will keep you gasping for air. Friday is a generational comedy movie of all time.
